About SeyiBabs

Known by the pen name Seyi Babs, he is an avid reader, eloquent speaker and prodigious penman.

He is the co-founder of Penpros.E, a creative company that offers top-notch writing services and tutilage with an hallmark of sped that leaves clients wowed.

He is a published author with four books. He is an SDG 4 advocate with a stern believe that one well-educated child can change the narative of Africa. He is a past fellow of the Millinium Campus Network, USA.

He currently serves as the Program Coordinator (Quality Education), Develop Africa Now Initiative, a youth and community based organization commited to promoting and advocation for sustainable development in rural and undeserved community in Africa.
Of recent vintage, I was appointed the Coordinator of Ugwumba Fellows at Ugwumba Center for Leadership Development in Africa, in charge of the Southwestern region region of Nigeria.
