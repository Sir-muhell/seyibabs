About SeyiBabs

Known by the pen name “Seyi Babs,” he is an avid reader, eloquent speaker and prodigious penman.

He served at the Head, Publicity and Communications' Department at Pison Group, one of Africa’s finest literary and leadership firm, from 2018 to 2021. He doubled as a Creative writing Educator at Pison Writing Academy; a subsidiary of Pison Group. He is the co-founder of PenPros.E, a creative company that offers top-notch writing services and tutelage with a hallmark of speed that leave clients wowed.

He is a writer and reporter for FUOYENEWS, a prominent campus blog in Ekiti State, Nigeria. His writing specialty ranges from content writing to ghostwriting, copy editing to copywriting.

An addicted writer cum Author, he has four books to his name; Cracked Pitcher, Everyone Buys You Just Don’t Want To Sell, Freelance Like A Free Lance, and 30 Things Ma-Da Never Taught Me –with the latter available on Amazon Bookstores (all books you want add to your reading list). During the lockdown, he showed over 500 writers how to turn their passion into profit and since then, the tally has risen.

In March 2020, he competed in the Hult Prize Foundations’ 2020 Challenge where he pitched a startup that combats plastic waste pollution. He was subsequently appointed a Director to help advance what the Hult Prize stands for in Nigeria.





What Else ?

He is an SDG 4 advocate with a stern belief that one well-educated child can change the narratives of Africa. He is a past fellow of the Millenium Campus Network, USA.

He currently serves as the Program Coordinator (Quality Education), Develop Africa Now Initiative, a youth and community based organization committed to promoting and advocating for sustainable development in rural and undeserved communities across Africa.

He is the founder of Agriternet.com, a 21st century AgricTeggKnowledgy company poised with revolutionalizing Agriculture in Nigeria.

With multivariate certifications in Professional writing, Leadership and Agripreneurship, he is an addicted social worker who is given to human capital development and creating impacts in my society, in line with the UN’s sustainable development goals.

He is an Ambassador for Plus Consulting Career Club; a club of professionals based in Maryland-USA, that helps African professionals grow Africa. In December 2020, he finished as a finalist in the Ugwumba Enterprise Challenge, where he pitched his startup Agriternet.com.

As a motivational actor, public speaker and serial entrepreneur, he believes the world is made of words and is committed to weaving them for God’s glory and man’s betterment. On a day he's not helping, he'd be seen drinking a little too much soda, while meditating.
